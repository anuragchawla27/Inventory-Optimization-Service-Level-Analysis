# 📦 Inventory Optimization & Service Level Analysis 

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes inventory performance using SQL and Power BI to identify overstock exposure, measure holding cost reduction, and validate service level reliability.

The primary business objective was to evaluate whether the company’s inventory strategy is cost-efficient while maintaining high product availability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Many companies maintain high inventory levels to avoid stockouts.  
However, excessive inventory leads to increased holding costs and capital lock-in.

This project answers:

- How much inventory is overstocked?
- What is the financial impact of excess inventory?
- Was cost reduced after optimization?
- Did the company face stockout risks?
- What is the trade-off between service level and holding cost?

## 📈 Final Results (Project Output)

- **30.01% inventory identified as overstock exposure**
- **₹62.78M excess holding cost detected**
- **12.34% total holding cost reduction achieved**
- **₹32M+ cost savings after optimization**
- **0 stockout events observed**
- **100% service level maintained**

## 🔎 Key Business Insight

The company operates with a conservative inventory strategy.  
While it successfully maintained 100% service level and avoided stockouts,  
approximately 30% of inventory represents overstock exposure, leading to significant holding cost impact.

This highlights a strategic trade-off between cost efficiency and service reliability.
